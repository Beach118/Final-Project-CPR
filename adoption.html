Adoption Process

To make the adoption official, a signed adoption contract and the adoption fee will be collected before the animal is placed in your care. If an animal has not yet been spayed or neutered, there is an additional $50 spay/neuter deposit. Once proof of the spay/neuter, the deposit will refunded. At the time of adoption, adopters should have a collar and leash/carrier.
